💡 Beginner Tips

  • Rest 60–90 seconds between sets
  • Move slow and controlled — don't rush reps
  • Fill the bag with books to adjust weight
  • If something hurts (not burns), stop and rest
  • Drink water before, during, and after
  • Sleep is where the gains happen — aim for 8hrs

📈 When to Progress

After 2–3 weeks, if the exercises feel easy, add more reps (aim for 15 before moving to a harder variation). Once knee push-ups feel easy, try incline push-ups with hands on a chair. Add more books to the bag as legs get stronger.

3 days/week · ~30 minutes per session · Full rest between workout days
